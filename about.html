About FreeCPTCodeFinder.com

FreeCPTCodeFinder.com is a free, interactive medical coding platform designed for physicians, surgeons, residents, PAs, NPs, and billing professionals. Built by a team of practicing surgeons and certified medical coders, it provides instant access to CPT codes, ICD-10 codes, wRVU values, and billing guidance — all from your browser, on any device.

Our Tools

  • CPT Code Decision Tree — Interactive specialty-based decision tree covering 30+ medical and surgical specialties. Navigate clinical questions to find the exact CPT code for your procedure.
  • wRVU Case Builder — Build multi-procedure cases with automatic smart ranking. The system orders procedures by billing hierarchy, applies MPPR rules, and handles add-on code exemptions.
  • E/M Level Calculator — MDM-based calculator for 99202-99215 with time-based billing option. Includes TCM calculator, modifier decision tool, and documentation templates.
  • AI CPT Assistant — In-browser AI powered by Qwen 2.5 that answers CPT coding and billing questions like an expert surgical coder. Runs entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device.
  • ICD-10 Browser — Common ICD-10 codes organized by system with search functionality.
  • Coding Blog — 44+ expert articles on CPT coding, modifiers, RVU optimization, and billing strategies across all specialties.

Our Mission

Medical coding shouldn't be a barrier to getting paid for the work you do. Too many physicians, residents, and APPs lack access to clear, practical coding guidance. We built FreeCPTCodeFinder.com to change that — free, fast, and accurate coding tools that work the way clinicians think.

Who We Serve

  • Surgeons — General, trauma, critical care, cardiothoracic, colorectal, HPB, surgical oncology, pediatric, and more
  • Primary Care Physicians — E/M optimization, preventive care coding, chronic care management

Data Sources

Our coding data is based on:

  • AMA CPT® Professional Edition — the standard reference for procedure coding
  • CMS Physician Fee Schedule (2026) — current wRVU values and conversion factors
  • CMS NCCI Edits — bundling rules and modifier guidance
  • ACS Operative Standards — surgical coding references from the American College of Surgeons
