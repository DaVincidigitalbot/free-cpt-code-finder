Graydon Stallard, DO, FACOS, FACS

Founder and Author

Graydon Stallard, DO, FACOS, FACS, is Board Certified in both General Surgery and Surgical Critical Care, with clinical expertise in general surgery, trauma, and acute care surgery. He serves as the Director of Medical Education at CaroMont Regional Medical Center and is an Assistant Professor of Surgery at VCOM, Edward Via College of Osteopathic Medicine in Spartanburg, South Carolina. He created FreeCPTCodeFinder.com to help students, residents, APPs, RNFAs, coders, and clinicians quickly find CPT codes for case logs and clinical workflows while also learning practical billing and documentation concepts.

Dr. Stallard serves as the developer and author for FreeCPTCodeFinder.com. Some educational articles and guides on FreeCPTCodeFinder.com may be drafted or organized with the assistance of AI tools. Content is edited before publication for clarity, educational value, and alignment with the site’s purpose. AI-assisted content does not replace professional judgment, official coding resources, payer policy, institutional guidance, or current CPT/CMS/NCCI references.

FreeCPTCodeFinder.com is intended for educational coding support. It is not medical advice, legal advice, compliance advice, or a guarantee of payer reimbursement. CPT, CMS, NCCI, institutional, and payer-specific rules may change and should always be verified.
