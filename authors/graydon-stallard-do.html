Graydon Stallard, DO, FACOS, FACS

Founder and Author

Graydon Stallard, DO, FACOS, FACS, is a surgeon specializing in General Surgery, Surgical Critical Care, and Trauma. He created FreeCPTCodeFinder.com to help residents, medical students, APPs, coders, and clinicians quickly find CPT codes for case logs and clinical documentation while also learning practical billing and documentation concepts that matter in practice.

Dr. Stallard serves as the developer and author for FreeCPTCodeFinder.com. Blog articles and educational guides are written by Dr. Stallard with the assistance of AI tools, then edited for clarity, clinical context, and educational usefulness before publication.

FreeCPTCodeFinder.com is intended for educational coding support. It is not medical advice, legal advice, compliance advice, or a guarantee of payer reimbursement. CPT, CMS, NCCI, institutional, and payer-specific rules may change and should always be verified.
