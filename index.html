CPT® is a registered trademark of the American Medical Association (AMA). All CPT codes, descriptions, and guidelines are © 2025 American Medical Association. All rights reserved.

ICD-10-CM codes are maintained by the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) under the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.

RVU data is sourced from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) Physician Fee Schedule. Values are approximate and based on the 2026 fee schedule. Actual reimbursement varies by payer, locality, and contract terms.

NCCI Bundling & Modifier Guidelines

Bundling rules are based on CMS National Correct Coding Initiative (NCCI) edits, updated quarterly. Always consult current NCCI edit files for definitive bundling determinations. Modifier usage depends on clinical documentation, payer policy, and specific circumstances.

RVU Calculator Disclaimer

The RVU calculator provides estimates based on national Medicare rates. Actual reimbursement depends on:

  • Geographic Practice Cost Indices (GPCI) for your locality
  • Individual payer contracts and fee schedules
  • Facility vs. non-facility setting
  • Provider participation status
  • Multiple procedure payment reduction (MPPR) policies

The conversion factor ($33.89 for 2026) is the CMS national rate and may not reflect your contracted rate.

Robotic Surgery Coding

Per AMA and ACS guidelines, robotic-assisted surgical procedures use the same CPT codes as laparoscopic equivalents. There is no separate CPT code or modifier for robotic assistance.
